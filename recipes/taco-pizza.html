Taco Pizza Recipe

Description

Enjoy the best of both worlds with this easy taco pizza recipe! A crispy pizza crust topped with seasoned beef, melty cheese, fresh veggies, and zesty taco flavors. Perfect for a fun family dinner or party snack!

Ingredients

  • 10 fluid ounces warm water
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • ¾ cup water
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix, divided
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) can fat-free refried beans
  • ⅓ cup salsa
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • 4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Add water, salt, oil, flour, and yeast to the bread machine in the listed order.
  2. Select the dough cycle and check the dough after a few minutes.
  3. If the dough is too dry, add water 1 tablespoon at a time until pliable but not sticky.
  4. In a small bowl, mix tomato paste, water, and ¾ of the taco seasoning.
  5. Stir in chili powder and cayenne pepper; set aside.
  6. In another bowl, combine refried beans, salsa, and onion; set aside.
  7. Cook ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned; drain excess fat.
  8. Season beef with the remaining taco seasoning and a little water; simmer briefly.
  9. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  10. Once the dough cycle finishes, remove and divide the dough in half.
  11. Pat each dough half into a 12-inch pan.
  12. Spread the bean mixture over the dough, followed by the tomato mixture.
  13. Sprinkle with seasoned beef and top with Cheddar cheese.
  14. Bake for about 15 minutes, turning pizzas halfway through, until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
